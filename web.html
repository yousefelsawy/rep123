Top 5 Video Games of 2025

  1. Hades 2:

    Hades 2 is a roguelike action role-playing video game developed and published by Supergiant Games. It is the sequel to Hades (2020) and was released on September 15, 2025, for Windows, macOS, and Nintendo Switch. The game follows melanoe, the princess of the Underworld, as she attempts to defeat chronus and save her family.

  2. Kingdom Come Deliverance 2:

    Kingdom Come Deliverance 2 is an action role-playing video game developed and published by Warhorse Studios. It is the sequel to Kingdom Come: Deliverance (2018) and was released on September 15, 2025, for Windows, macOS, and Linux. The game follows the story of a young man named Henry, who must send a message to form an alliance for a civil war.

  3. Clair Obscure expedition 33:

    Clair Obscure expedition 33 is an action-adventure video game developed and published by a hypothetical studio. The game follows the story of a young explorer named Clair, who must navigate the mysteries of an ancient civilization while uncovering the truth behind a series of strange occurrences.

  4. Death stranding 2:

    Death stranding 2 is a survival-action video game developed and published by Kojima Productions. It is the sequel to Death stranding (2019) and was released on September 15, 2025, for PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 5. The game follows the story of Sam, who must navigate a post-apocalyptic world while dealing with the consequences of his actions.

  5. hollow knight: silk song:

    Hollow Knight: Silk Song is an action-adventure video game developed and published by Team17. It is the sequel to Hollow Knight (2017) and was released on September 15, 2025, for Windows, macOS, and Linux. The game follows the story of a young knight named Hollow Knight as he navigates the mysterious world of Hallownest.
